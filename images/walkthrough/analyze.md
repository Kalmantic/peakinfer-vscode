## Analyze Your Code

PeakInfer detects LLM inference calls in:
- **TypeScript/JavaScript** - OpenAI, Anthropic, LangChain
- **Python** - openai, anthropic, langchain
- **Go** - go-openai, anthropic-sdk
- **Rust** - async-openai

**Keyboard shortcut:** `Cmd+Alt+P` (Mac) or `Ctrl+Alt+P` (Windows/Linux)

You'll see issues highlighted directly in your code!
